生效日期：2026 年 7 月 8 日。最后更新：2026 年 7 月 8 日。

PhotoTransfer Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026. Last updated: July 8, 2026.

概述

PhotoTransfer 尊重你的隐私。应用的核心功能是在你主动选择文件或文件夹后，将其中的照片、视频以及可匹配的实况照片素材导入到系统相册。

PhotoTransfer 不会收集、出售、共享或上传你的个人数据、照片、视频或文件。

我们访问哪些数据

  • 你选择的文件或文件夹：应用只会读取你通过系统文件选择器授权的位置，用于扫描和导入媒体文件。
  • 照片图库：应用请求照片图库权限，用于向系统相册添加照片和视频、创建或复用 PhotoTransfer 相册，并在开启跳过重复项目时检查 PhotoTransfer 相册中的已有媒体。
  • 本地设置：应用会在设备本地保存你的偏好设置，例如相册命名方式、是否跳过重复项目、是否跳过视频及视频大小阈值。
  • 导入进度：应用会在界面、锁屏实时活动或灵动岛中显示当前导入进度、文件名、成功数、失败数和传输速度等状态信息。

我们如何使用这些数据

  • 读取你授权的文件或文件夹，识别照片、视频和可合并为实况照片的素材。
  • 将媒体导入系统照片图库，并按你的设置创建或复用相册。
  • 检测 PhotoTransfer 相册中的重复项目，以减少重复导入。
  • 在本机显示导入进度和结果。

不会进行的行为

  • 不会将你的照片、视频或文件上传到开发者服务器。
  • 不会出售或共享你的个人数据。
  • 不会用于广告追踪。
  • 不会使用第三方分析 SDK 收集使用行为。
  • 不会在未经你通过系统授权的情况下读取文件或照片图库。

数据存储

导入的照片和视频保存在你的系统照片图库中。应用偏好设置保存在设备本地。PhotoTransfer 不运营用于接收或存储用户媒体内容的服务器。

权限说明

  • 照片图库权限：用于向相册添加导入内容、创建或复用 PhotoTransfer 相册，以及根据设置检查已导入项目。
  • 文件访问权限：通过系统文件选择器授予，仅用于读取你选择的文件或文件夹。
  • 实时活动：用于在锁屏或灵动岛展示导入进度。显示内容来自本机导入任务。

关于“无线数据”系统提示

在 iOS 26.4 及更高版本中，部分用户在首次点击应用内的文本输入框时，可能会看到系统弹出“允许‘PhotoTransfer’使用无线数据？”的提示。这是 Apple 系统在 iOS 26.4 引入的已知问题，由系统文本输入组件触发，与 PhotoTransfer 无关。

PhotoTransfer 不包含任何联网代码，不会请求、发送或接收任何网络数据。你可以放心选择任意选项，应用的全部功能均在设备本地完成，不依赖网络。有关该系统问题的官方说明，请参见 Apple 开发者论坛：https://developer.apple.com/forums/thread/822198。

儿童隐私

PhotoTransfer 不面向儿童收集个人数据，也不会主动收集任何年龄信息。

政策变更

如果未来版本增加会影响隐私的数据处理方式，本页面会更新相应说明，并修改“最后更新”日期。

Overview

PhotoTransfer respects your privacy. The app is built to import photos, videos, and matching Live Photo resources from files or folders that you explicitly select into the system photo library.

PhotoTransfer does not collect, sell, share, or upload your personal data, photos, videos, or files.

Data the app may access

  • Files or folders you select: The app reads only locations that you authorize through the system file picker, for the purpose of scanning and importing media.
  • Photo library: The app requests photo library permission to add imported photos and videos, create or reuse PhotoTransfer albums, and check existing media in the PhotoTransfer album when duplicate skipping is enabled.
  • Local settings: The app stores preferences on your device, such as album naming, duplicate skipping, video skipping, and video size thresholds.
  • Import progress: The app may show local import status, including file name, progress, successful items, failed items, and transfer speed in the app, Live Activities, or Dynamic Island.

How the data is used

  • To read authorized files or folders and identify photos, videos, and resources that can be rebuilt as Live Photos.
  • To import media into the system photo library and create or reuse albums according to your settings.
  • To detect duplicate items in the PhotoTransfer album when the duplicate skipping option is enabled.
  • To display import progress and results locally on your device.

What the app does not do

  • It does not upload your photos, videos, or files to developer-operated servers.
  • It does not sell or share your personal data.
  • It does not use your data for advertising tracking.
  • It does not use third-party analytics SDKs to collect usage behavior.
  • It does not read files or the photo library without system-granted permission.

Data storage

Imported photos and videos are stored in your system photo library. App preferences are stored locally on your device. PhotoTransfer does not operate servers for receiving or storing user media.

Permissions

  • Photo library permission: Used to add imported content, create or reuse PhotoTransfer albums, and check imported items according to your settings.
  • File access permission: Granted through the system file picker and used only to read files or folders you select.
  • Live Activities: Used to show import progress on the Lock Screen or Dynamic Island. The displayed information comes from the local import task.

About the "wireless data" system prompt

On iOS 26.4 and later, some users may see a system alert "Allow “PhotoTransfer” to use wireless data?" the first time they tap a text field in the app. This is a known iOS issue introduced in iOS 26.4, triggered by the system text-input component, and is not related to PhotoTransfer.

PhotoTransfer contains no networking code and does not request, send, or receive any network data. You may choose any option safely; all app functionality runs locally on your device and does not depend on a network connection. For Apple's official discussion of this system issue, see the Apple Developer Forums: https://developer.apple.com/forums/thread/822198.

Children's privacy

PhotoTransfer does not knowingly collect personal data from children and does not request age information.

Changes to this policy

If a future version changes how privacy-related data is handled, this page will be updated and the “Last updated” date will be revised.
