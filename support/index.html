常见问题

Frequently Asked Questions

为什么需要照片图库权限？

PhotoTransfer 需要照片图库权限来添加导入的照片和视频、创建或复用 PhotoTransfer 相册，并在开启“跳过已导入项目”时检查应用相册中的已有媒体。

为什么需要从“文件”App 选择文件或文件夹？

iOS 只允许应用读取你明确授权的位置。PhotoTransfer 只会读取你通过系统文件选择器选择的文件或文件夹。

实况照片如何合并？

应用会扫描可导入项目，尝试匹配照片与对应的 MOV 文件，并把匹配结果导入为系统相册中的实况照片。

导入时可以离开 App 吗？

导入过程中请尽量保持 PhotoTransfer 运行。支持实时活动的设备可以在锁屏或灵动岛查看进度，但长时间后台运行仍可能受系统限制。

如何减少重复导入？

在设置中开启“跳过已重复导入的项目”。应用会检查 PhotoTransfer 相册中的已有媒体，帮助减少重复导入。

Why does PhotoTransfer need photo library permission?

PhotoTransfer needs photo library permission to add imported photos and videos, create or reuse PhotoTransfer albums, and check existing media when duplicate skipping is enabled.

Why do I need to pick files or folders from Files?

iOS only allows apps to read locations you explicitly authorize. PhotoTransfer reads only files or folders selected through the system file picker.

How are Live Photos rebuilt?

The app scans importable items, matches still photos with corresponding MOV files, and imports matched pairs as Live Photos in the system photo library.

Can I leave the app while importing?

Please keep PhotoTransfer running while importing. Devices with Live Activities can show progress on the Lock Screen or Dynamic Island, but long-running background work may still be limited by the system.

How do I reduce duplicate imports?

Turn on “Skip already imported items” in Settings. The app checks existing media in the PhotoTransfer album to help reduce repeated imports.
